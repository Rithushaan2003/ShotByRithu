About Me

The Journey Behind the Lens

I’m Rithushaan — but everyone calls me Rithu. I’m a photographer based in London, capturing portraits, landscapes, and lifestyle moments with a clean, cinematic style.

Photography started as a hobby, something I did for fun. Over time, it became a passion — a way to freeze emotions, tell stories, and create something meaningful. I’m still growing, still learning, and still building my portfolio one frame at a time.

Whether it’s birthdays, portraits, events, or creative shoots, I aim to capture moments that feel real — moments you can look back on and remember exactly how it felt.

“Every photo is a memory — and every memory deserves to be captured properly.”

If you’d like to work together, collaborate, or just say hi, feel free to reach out through my contact page or Instagram.
